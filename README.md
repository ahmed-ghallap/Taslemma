# **Taselmma Platform**

**Taselmma** is a smart digital platform designed to simplify and organize the process of delivering, receiving, and managing official academic and administrative documents between students, supervisors, and institutions.

The platform acts as a secure digital bridge that replaces traditional paper-based procedures with a fast, transparent, and trackable online workflow.
